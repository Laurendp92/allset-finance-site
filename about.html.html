About Us

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you clear numbers, practical insights, and peace of mind so you can focus on running your business.

We are led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting, and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from one-person consultancies to international teams — we know the value of straightforward, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your financial compass — guiding you through the day-to-day and preparing you for the road ahead.

What Makes Us Different

  • Plain language: We explain your numbers in a way that makes sense — no jargon, no clutter.
  • Smart tools + human touch: We combine modern accounting software with hands-on expertise, so your books are accurate and your insights are meaningful.
  • Forward-looking support: Beyond compliance, we help you plan ahead with dashboards, forecasts, and investor-ready reports.
