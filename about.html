About Us

At Allset Finance, we go beyond the numbers. Proudly Irish and based in Cork, we bring the expertise of a Chartered Accountant with audit and financial advisory experience to small and growing businesses. We’re here to give you clear financial insights, peace of mind about compliance, and the confidence to focus on what you do best — running your business.

Our approach is straightforward and practical — no jargon, no confusion. Whether you’re starting out or planning for growth, we tailor our support so it fits your stage and helps you move forward with clarity.

Based in Ireland. Supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

We believe finance should do more than meet compliance. It should give business owners clarity, confidence, and the freedom to make better decisions. That’s why we combine professional expertise with practical tools and advice, helping clients not just stay on track but grow with purpose.

What Makes Us Different

  • Reliable: Accurate bookkeeping and compliance, delivered on time.
  • Support at every step: From setup to ongoing financial guidance, we’re with you all the way.
  • Scales with you: Flexible support that grows as your business grows.
  • True partnership: We work alongside you, not just for you.
  • Future-ready: Forecasts, dashboards, and insights to prepare you for what’s next.
