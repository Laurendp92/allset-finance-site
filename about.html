About Us

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you tidy numbers, practical insights, and the peace of mind to focus on running your business.

We are proudly an Irish-owned company, led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from solo consultants to international teams — we understand the value of clear, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your trusted financial compass — guiding you through the day-to-day while preparing you for the road ahead. We aim to simplify finance, reduce stress, and empower you with the tools and advice you need to grow confidently.

We do this by combining organised, timely bookkeeping with clear reporting and forward-looking guidance so you always know: where your business stands, what next month will look like, and what decisions will help you reach your goals.

  • Practical clarity: Concise monthly reports and KPI dashboards that focus on what matters.
  • Predictability: Rolling cashflow forecasts and reminders so deadlines never sneak up on you.
  • Growth-ready: Investor packs, reforecasting and cap-table support when you need to scale or fundraise.

What Makes Us Different

We combine plain language, modern tools, and Chartered Accountant oversight so your books are accurate and your insights are useful. Beyond compliance, we build simple dashboards and forecasts that let you make confident decisions.

  • Plain language: We explain your numbers clearly — no jargon, no clutter.
  • Smart tools + expert oversight: We use Xero, AutoEntry/Dext and Looker Studio / Power BI, combined with CA review for accuracy.
  • Forward-looking support: Dashboards, rolling forecasts and investor-ready reporting to help you plan and pitch.
  • Personalised partnership: We adapt our approach to your company stage — solo consultant, growing startup or established SME.
  • Responsive & practical: Quick answers when you need them and actionable advice that saves time and reduces risk.
