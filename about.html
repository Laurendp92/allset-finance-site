About Allset

At Allset Finance Solutions, I believe accounting should give business owners confidence—not confusion.

I'm a Chartered Accountant based in Ballincollig, Cork, and I founded Allset to provide a more personal, straightforward approach to bookkeeping, accounting and financial support.

I started Allset Finance Solutions because I wanted to offer business owners a more personal, approachable alternative to traditional accounting firms—one where clear communication, long-term relationships and practical advice come first.

Whether you're a sole trader, contractor, startup or growing business, you'll receive reliable support and a service tailored to your needs. When you work with Allset, you'll always deal directly with me, giving you consistent support from someone who understands your business and is invested in helping it succeed.

My aim is simple: to give you the clarity and confidence to focus on what you do best—running your business.

The Story Behind Allset

Hi, I'm Lauren du Preez, founder of Allset Finance Solutions.

After qualifying as a Chartered Accountant, I built my career working with businesses of all sizes, gaining experience across audit, accounting, tax and financial reporting.

I began my career at leading professional services firms, including PwC and EY, where I developed a strong understanding of how businesses operate and the importance of reliable financial information.

"Over time, I realised that what I enjoyed most wasn't simply preparing accounts — it was building relationships with business owners and helping them understand their finances with confidence."

That realisation inspired me to create Allset Finance Solutions — a practice built around personal support, clear communication and practical advice.

Today, I support sole traders, contractors, startups and growing businesses across Ireland, helping them stay organised, compliant and prepared for the future.

Our Mission

Allset Finance Solutions' mission is to give Irish businesses the financial clarity and structure they need to grow with confidence. We combine professional expertise with practical, reliable support — always delivered with care, precision and integrity.

We believe every business deserves numbers they can trust, systems that work, and guidance that feels calm, clear and genuinely helpful. Our goal is to make your financial world easier to manage, easier to understand, and easier to build on.

Clarity. Confidence. Growth — the Allset way.
