About Us

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you tidy numbers, practical insights, and the peace of mind to focus on running your business.

We are proudly an Irish-owned company, led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from solo consultants to international teams — we understand the value of clear, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your trusted financial compass — keeping the day-to-day on track while helping you prepare for what's ahead. We simplify finance so you can run your business with confidence: tidy books, clear reports, and guidance that helps you make better decisions.

We deliver:

  • Practical clarity: Short monthly reports and KPI dashboards highlighting only what matters so you can act quickly.
  • Predictability: Rolling cashflow forecasts, reminders and schedules so VAT, payroll and tax dates never catch you out.
  • Growth-ready support: Investor packs, reforecasting and cap-table help when you need to scale, fundraise or apply for grants.
  • Human access: Real conversations and timely responses — not late, generic emails.

What Makes Us Different

We combine plain language with modern tools and Chartered Accountant oversight to give you reliable numbers and actionable insight.

  • Plain language: We explain your numbers without jargon — clear takeaways and next steps, not long technical reports.
  • Smart tools + expert oversight: We use Xero / AutoEntry / Dext and Power BI / Looker Studio for fast, accurate workflows — then a Chartered Accountant reviews the outputs so insights are dependable.
  • Forward-looking support: Rolling forecasts, scenario modelling and investor-ready packs so you’re prepared for opportunities and risks.
  • Personalised partnership: We adapt our level of support to your stage — from solo consultants to fast-growth startups and small teams.
  • Responsive & practical: Prompt, useful advice — not busywork. We prioritise the actions that reduce risk and free time for your business.
