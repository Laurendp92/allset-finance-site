About Us

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you tidy numbers, practical insights, and the peace of mind to focus on running your business.

We are proudly an Irish-owned company, led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting, and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from solo consultants to international teams — we understand the value of clear, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your trusted financial compass — guiding you through the day-to-day while helping you prepare for what’s ahead. We aim to simplify finance, reduce stress, and empower you with the tools and advice you need to grow confidently.

  • Practical clarity: Clear monthly reports and KPI dashboards that focus on what matters — explained in plain English.
  • Predictability: Rolling cashflow forecasts, reminders, and proactive alerts so deadlines never sneak up on you.
  • Growth-ready: Investor packs, reforecasting, and fundraise-ready numbers when you need to scale or seek funding.

What Makes Us Different

We combine plain language with modern technology and Chartered Accountant oversight to deliver finance that helps you run and grow your business.

  • Plain language: We explain your numbers in a way that makes sense — no jargon, no clutter.
  • Smart tools + human touch: We use modern accounting tools (Xero, AutoEntry/Dext, Power BI / Looker Studio) and back them with hands-on Chartered Accountant review so your books are accurate and your insights meaningful.
  • Forward-looking support: Beyond compliance, we provide rolling forecasts, KPI dashboards, and investor-ready reporting to help you plan ahead.
  • Personalized partnership: We adapt our approach to your size and stage — whether you’re a solo consultant, fast-growing startup, or established SME.
  • Always accessible: We answer queries promptly and proactively — so financial clarity is available when you need it.

Ready to tidy your numbers?

Tell us a bit about your business and we'll send a clear proposal.
