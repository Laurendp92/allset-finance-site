About Us
Allset Finance Solutions

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you tidy numbers, practical insights, and the peace of mind to focus on running your business.

We are proudly an Irish–owned company, led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from solo consultants to international teams — we understand the value of clear, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your trusted financial compass — guiding you through the day-to-day while preparing you for the road ahead. We aim to simplify finance, reduce stress, and empower you with the tools and advice you need to grow confidently.

Practical clarity: Clear monthly reports and KPI dashboards that focus on what matters.

Predictability: Rolling cashflow forecasts and reminders so deadlines never sneak up on you.

Growth-ready: Investor packs and reforecasting when you need to scale or fundraise.

Long-term partnership: We support you at every stage, from startup to expansion.

What Makes Us Different

We combine plain language with modern technology and professional oversight to deliver financial support that truly makes sense for your business.

Plain language: We explain your numbers in a way that makes sense — no jargon, no clutter, just clarity.

Smart tools + expert oversight: We use trusted software (Xero, AutoEntry/Dext, Power BI/Looker Studio) combined with Chartered Accountant review, so your records are accurate and insights reliable.

Forward-looking support: Beyond compliance, we provide rolling forecasts, dashboards, and investor-ready packs that help you plan for growth.

Personalized partnership: We adapt our approach to your stage and industry — whether you’re a solo consultant, fast-growing startup, or established SME.

Always accessible: You get responsive support when you need it, not weeks later — because financial clarity shouldn’t wait.
