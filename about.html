About Us

At Allset Finance Solutions, we believe finance shouldn’t be confusing or overwhelming. Whether you’re a contractor, startup, or growing business, our goal is simple: to give you clear numbers, practical insights, and peace of mind so you can focus on running your business.

We are proudly an Irish-owned company, led by a Chartered Accountant with years of experience in bookkeeping, tax compliance, financial reporting, and advisory. Having worked with businesses of all sizes — from solo consultants to international teams — we understand the value of clear, reliable finance support.

Based in Ireland, supporting clients nationwide.

Our Mission

To be your trusted financial compass — guiding you through the day-to-day while preparing you for the road ahead. We aim to simplify finance, reduce stress, and empower you with the tools and advice you need to grow confidently.

  • Practical clarity: Clear monthly reports and a KPI dashboard that focus on what matters.
  • Predictability: Rolling cashflow forecasts and reminders so deadlines never sneak up on you.
  • Growth-ready: Investor packs and reforecasting when you need to scale or fundraise.

What Makes Us Different

Plain language

We explain your numbers in a way that makes sense — no jargon, no clutter.

Smart tools + human touch

We combine modern accounting software (Xero, Dext/AutoEntry, Power BI/Looker Studio) with hands-on expertise to keep your books accurate and useful.

Forward-looking support

Beyond compliance, we help you plan with dashboards, forecasts and investor-ready reports so you make confident decisions.
