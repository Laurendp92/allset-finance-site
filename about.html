About Allset

At Allset Finance Solutions, I believe accounting should give business owners confidence—not confusion.

I'm a Chartered Accountant based in Ballincollig, Cork, and I founded Allset to provide a more personal, straightforward approach to bookkeeping, accounting and financial support. My goal is to help businesses stay organised, understand their numbers and make informed decisions without unnecessary jargon or complexity.

Whether you're a sole trader, contractor, startup or growing business, you'll receive practical advice, reliable support and a service tailored to your needs. When you work with Allset, you'll always deal directly with me, giving you consistent support from someone who takes the time to understand your business and is genuinely invested in helping it succeed.

From bookkeeping and payroll to tax, financial reporting and business support, my aim is to give you the clarity and confidence to focus on what you do best—running your business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give Irish businesses the financial clarity and structure they need to grow with confidence. We combine professional expertise with practical, reliable support — always delivered with care, precision and integrity.

We believe every business deserves numbers they can trust, systems that work, and guidance that feels calm, clear and genuinely helpful. Our goal is to make your financial world easier to manage, easier to understand, and easier to build on.

Clarity. Confidence. Growth — the Allset way.
